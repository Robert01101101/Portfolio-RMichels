Community Guidelines

Last updated: July 25, 2024

Tourguide hopes to foster a positive community. As part of that please consider the community guidelines when adding content or interacting with content and community members.

We also encourage you to review our Terms of Service and Privacy Policy.

Do's

  • Have fun: Explore and enjoy the world to its fullest! There is so much beauty in every place and we want you to discover it.
  • Be Respectful: Treat other users with kindness and respect. Appreciate the diversity and different viewpoints within our community.
  • Stay Safe: Follow safety guidelines while traveling and share tips that help others stay safe. Report any suspicious activities or content.
  • Share Quality Content: Share high-quality, relevant content that can help and inspire fellow travelers. Ensure your posts are informative and enjoyable.

    • Don'ts

  • Inappropriate Content: Do not post or share any explicit, sexual, or pornographic content, or content depicting or promoting violence.
  • Offensive Language: Avoid using offensive, hateful, or discriminatory language in your posts, comments, or messages.
  • Harassment: Do not harass, bully, or act in a way that can be perceived as threatening, intimidating, or demeaning to others. Treat all users with respect and dignity.
