Tourguide Account Deletion

Deleting your Account deletes all of your personal information such as your user profile, and all of your contributions including tours you have created. It also deletes records of communications with you, such as email notifications.

If you would like to delete your account and associated data, follow these instructions:

  1. Open the Tourguide App
  2. Sign In (if you have been signed in for a while, you may be required to sign out and then back in before you can proceed with account deletion)
  3. Navigate to: Profile -> Profile Settings -> Delete Account
  4. Type "DELETE" and then press the Delete Button to permanently delete your account and all associated data.

If you have any trouble with deleting your account data, please reach out to contact@tourguide.rmichels.com
